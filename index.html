Gender Inequality in Transportation and Work-Life Balance

Female faculty members at UCLA, encompassing both caregivers and non-caregivers, have distinct commutes and the accompanying responsibilities that shape their work-life balance. This interactive map experience empowers users to delve into the intricacies of work-life balance and gender inequalities in transportation, gaining valuable insights from the perspectives of female faculty members at UCLA who identify as caregivers or non-caregivers.
