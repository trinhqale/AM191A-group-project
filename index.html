Girls Just Want to Have Fun:

Gender Inequality in Transportation and Work-Life Balance

Women are more significantly impacted by challenges in regards to transportation and traveling behavior due to underlying factors, such as societal and cultural roles as caregivers. This project is to provide a platform where female faculty at UCLA—both caregivers and single women—can anonymously share their experiences with their commute and work-life balance.
